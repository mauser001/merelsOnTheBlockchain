Installation tuturial to play Merels for free on the Testnet

  • Install Metamask
  • Metamask will be accessable through the fix icon on the top right of your browser
  • (optinal) Add new accout
  • Add the test network to your metmask (icon on the top right > settings > network > new network)
    • Name: Matic Mumbai Testnet
    • RPC-URL: {{_blockchainService.connection.chainRpc}}
    • Chain ID: {{_blockchainService.connection.chainId}}
    • Currency Symbol: {{_blockchainService.connection.currencySymbol}}
    • Block-Explorer URL: {{_blockchainService.connection.blockExplorerUrl}}
  • Make sure the new network is selected in your metamask
  • Copy the public key of your (new) metamask account to your clipboard
  • Get FREE Test-Matic with your public key from Matic Faucet
  • Connect you account with Metamask now!
    • Open your metamask
    • Click on the account you want to play with
    • Confirm the connection
